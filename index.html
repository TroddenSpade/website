Behrooz Nasihatkon

nasihatkon at-sign kntu dot ac dot ir

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Engineering, Khajeh Nasir University of Technology, Tehran, Iran. My areas of interest include Computer Vision, Multiple View Geometry, Biomedical Image and Signal Processing, Statistical Pattern Recognition, Graphical Models, Riemannian Geometry, Compressive Sensing, Optimization and Statistical Modeling.

I am Co-Founder of Rahbin Nasir Sanat company where we are aiming at the promotion of the safety level of transportation industry with the use of smart systems. Rahbin Nasir Sanat has begun its activities in the field of design and implementation of advanced driver assistance systems.
